Night Safari Visitors

Problem statement

In 2020, the world's first nocturnal zoo, the Night Safari in Singapore, attractedapproximately 343 thousand visitors. The Night Safari is part of the four wildlife-centered leisure attractions in Singapore, which includes the:

  • Singapore zoo
  • Jurong Birdpark
  • River Safari

Due to the COVID 19 pandemic, the Night Safari was closed from April to June 2020, and has since then operated at limited capacity under safety measures
